ECONOMIC CONSTITUTION OF ALTAN NETWORK
Version 2

Article 1. Network Sovereignty
The ALTAN network is a sovereign digital infrastructure.
All transactions, fees, and settlements occur exclusively within ALTAN.

Article 2. ALTAN Currency
ALTAN is the native currency of the network.
Issuance is performed exclusively by the Altan Central Bank
based on Khural decisions.

Article 3. Network Fee
A unified network fee of 0.03% applies to all transactions.
100% of the fee is routed to the INOMAD KHURAL Treasury.
